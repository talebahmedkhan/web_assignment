Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is a successful business executive who is best known for being the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Google Inc. He also heads its parent company Alphabet Inc. Born and raised in India. He began his professional career with McKinsey & Company before joining Google in 2004. He worked there for about a decade, heading various projects and rising through the ranks

Sundar Pichai Education

He completed hid B.Tech. in Metallurgical & Materials Engineering from IIT Kharagpur in 1993 and completed his Master’s i.e., M.S. in Materials Science & Engineering from Stanford University in 1995 and then worked as Materials Engineer at Applied Materials for 5 years from 1995 to 2000. And he was graduated as an MBA from The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania in 2002.
